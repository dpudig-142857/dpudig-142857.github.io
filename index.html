What is hidden in the metabolomics of the tear film of the eye?

Description:
As part of my COMP3900 Capstone Project, I worked with a team of six to build a web-based visualization tool designed for optometrists. The tool helps users explore how different metabolites, compounds, and biological pathways are connected in the tear film of the eye - useful for both research and clinical insights.

I was responsible for the UX/UI design and took on the role of SCRUM master, coordinating development and keeping the project running smoothly across sprints.

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, SQL, JSON

Tax Calculator

Description:
I developed a custom tax calculator as a simple web-based tool for Cartland Law, designed to streamline property tax calculations. Users can enter landholder details, property addresses, land values, and ownership percentages, and the calculator automatically distributes tax obligations accordingly.

This was one of the first websites I ever built. At the time, I was just learning HTML and CSS, so the design is minimal and functional. While basic in appearance, the tool serves its purpose effectively and gave me valuable early experience working on real-world problems for a client.
